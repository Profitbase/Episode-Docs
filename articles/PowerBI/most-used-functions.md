
**CAGR(beginningValue, endingValue, years)**  
Calculates compound annual growth rate.

**YoY(pastValue, presentValue)**  
Calculates year-over-year growth.

**IF(condition, trueValue, falseValue)**  
Works like the Excel IF function.
